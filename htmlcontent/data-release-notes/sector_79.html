    Sector Notes

    Noted Issue Description
    Spacecraft pointing: The pointing in Sector 79 was set at +83.5 degrees in ecliptic latitude so that Camera 3 points near the North Ecliptic Pole. Cameras 1 and 4 were used in Orbits 165a and 166a. Camera 4 was used for guiding in Orbits 165b and 166b.
    Scattered light: In Sector 79, the Earth introduces scattered light signals in the middle of Orbits 165b and 166b. The Moon impacts Camera 1 near the middle of Orbit 165a.
