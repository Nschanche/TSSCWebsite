    Noted Issue Description
    Spacecraft pointing: The pointing in Sector 80 was set at +55.5 degrees in ecliptic latitude. Camera 4 was pointed at the North Ecliptic Pole. Cameras 1 and 4 were used in Orbits 167a and 168a. Camera 4 was used for guiding in Orbits 167b and 168b.
    Scattered light: In Sector 80, the Earth introduces scattered light signals towards the end of each orbit, and the Moon impacts Cameras 1 and 2 near the middle of Orbit 167b.
